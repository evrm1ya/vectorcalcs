About

V is for Vector was created to assist college trigonometry and calculus III students in their study of vectors and applications involving vectors. It was not created as a substitute for what can be taught in class by teacher and textbook. There are already many fine websites that explain vectors in great detail.

To the student:

Use V is for Vector as a tool to check your work and build confidence as you learn about vectors. Minimal definitions are provided to help you understand the procedure of vector calculations.
