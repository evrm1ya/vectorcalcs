About

Vector Calcs was created to assist calculus and pre-calculus students in their study of vectors. It is a collection of vector operation calculators. Each calculator contains a minimal definition describing how to perform the calculation; however, the definition is not intended to describe any of the deeper mathematical concepts related to the operation. Use the calculators to check your work and as an aid to help you gain an understanding of vectors.
